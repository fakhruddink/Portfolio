I’m Fakhruddin Kuwala, a Senior iOS Developer, Team Lead, and product builder who loves turning ideas into real, scalable products. My journey started in 2014 with Objective-C, and since then I’ve been fully immersed in the Apple ecosystem, helping bring more than 25 apps from concept to the App Store.


I don’t just write code. I build products. From shaping the first idea to designing the architecture, leading teams, and shipping polished experiences, I’ve been hands-on at every stage of the product journey. I care deeply about performance, scalability, and crafting experiences that users actually enjoy using.


Alongside development, I work closely with founders, stakeholders, and clients to define vision, refine requirements, and solve real business problems with technology. This mix of product thinking and engineering helps me bridge the gap between what’s technically possible and what makes sense for the business.


My core stack is Swift, SwiftUI, and modern iOS frameworks, with strong experience in e-commerce platforms, payment integrations, and growth-focused App Store optimization. I’m passionate about building meaningful products, scaling ideas into businesses, and creating technology that makes a real impact.
